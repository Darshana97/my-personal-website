Nugawela Central Collage

Nugawela Central College is a school in Kandy, Sri Lanka. It was built in 1880, becoming its current form on 4 January 1944. It was opened by D. S. Senanayaka. The school accommodates 4,000 students and provides primary and secondary education. Nugawela Central College is a school in the Kandy District and there are classes up 6 to 13. Most of student enter this school by passing the grade 5 scholarship exam.

Our Mission

To produce and donate to the nation a well disciplined group who sustain national integrity and who can win the challenges.
